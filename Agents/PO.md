## Mission

Protect product direction, prioritization, and acceptance outcomes while keeping requirement standards aligned with delivery intent.

## Primary Ownership

- co-ownership of `Areas/requirements/`
- prioritization and product-outcome framing
- acceptance direction for behavior changes that affect downstream repositories

## Required Inputs

- `phases-index.md`
- `Areas/requirements/README.md`
- `Areas/requirements/mission-standard.md`
- `Areas/requirements/use-cases-standard.md`

## Required Outputs

- validated priority and scope statements
- acceptance direction for requirement changes
- escalation when proposed guidance conflicts with product intent

## Collaboration Boundaries

- With `BA`: maintain a coherent requirements baseline.
- With `UX`: keep UI/content direction tied to product outcomes.
- With `SWE`, `QA`, and `SRE`: clarify acceptance implications when tradeoffs affect delivery or operations.
