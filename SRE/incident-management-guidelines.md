## Required Sections

1. Incident Classification
2. Triage and Escalation
3. Communication Protocol
4. Mitigation Actions
5. Root Cause Follow-up
6. Post-Incident Verification
7. Preventive Action Tracking

## Rules

- Incident severity must be assigned using explicit impact criteria.
- Escalation paths must be deterministic and time-bound.
- Communication must include impact, scope, and current mitigation status.
- Incident closure requires RCA and verified preventive actions.
- If evidence is missing, state `Evidence not available`.

## Quality Checklist

- Severity and owner are recorded.
- Mitigation timeline is documented.
- RCA and post-fix verification are linked.
- Preventive actions have owners and due dates.
